Mother Teresa

Mother Teresa helps the homeless in a charity house

Here's a timeline of Mother Teresa's life:

  • 1910 — Agnes Gonja Boyadzhiu was born in the city of Skopje in the Ottoman Empire
  • 1919 — the father of Agnes, who was 9 years old at that time, dies under unclear circumstances.
  • 1928 — Agnes Gonja Boyadzhiu graduated from high school in Skopje;
  • 1937 — Agnes took monastic vows and became the director of the Bengali department at St. Anne's School.
  • 1946 — received permission from the leadership of the order to help the poor and destitute of Calcutta;
  • 1952 — Mother Teresa opened the first house for the dying — "Nirmal Hriday"
  • 1979 — Mother Teresa becomes the winner of the Nobel Peace Prize;
  • 1997 – Mother Teresa's death day;
